Products & Services

TradeBit

TradeBit is a specialized financial module of the SynaBrain Intelligent Decision System, designed specifically for trading, investment, and risk management.

  • Market Sentiment Analysis: Real-time analysis of market news, social media, and trading data
  • Portfolio Optimization: Automatic portfolio adjustment based on risk preferences and market predictions
  • Risk Alerts: Identification of potential risk factors with early warnings
  • Trading Strategy Backtesting: Simulation of historical market environments to evaluate trading strategy effectiveness

SynaForge

SynaForge is an open AI developer community platform dedicated to connecting developers with enterprise needs to jointly promote AI application innovation.

  • Resource Sharing: Providing models, datasets, development tools, and other resources
  • Knowledge Exchange: Expert experience sharing and technical problem solving
  • Project Collaboration: Developers can participate in actual commercial project development
  • Innovation Incubation: Supporting promising AI innovation projects from concept to implementation

SynaCore

SynaCore is a one-stop AI capability integration platform for medium and large enterprises, helping businesses build their own AI central system to achieve comprehensive business intelligence.

  • Knowledge Graph Construction: Automated enterprise knowledge extraction and relational analysis
  • Process Intelligence: Identification and optimization of inefficient segments in business processes
  • Intelligent Customer Service Engine: Support for multilingual, multi-channel intelligent customer service
  • Decision Support System: Data-driven decision support for management

OmniAI

OmniAI is an innovative AI service aggregation platform providing users with a one-stop experience to access multiple AI tools and services.

  • Multi-Engine Access: Integration of mainstream AI services in the market with one-click switching
  • Unified Interface: Providing consistent user experience and interaction methods
  • Capability Stacking: Supporting collaborative work between multiple AI engines for complementary advantages
  • Personalized Recommendations: Learning user preferences to recommend the most suitable AI services

Lumina

Lumina is a mental health service application that integrates AI technology, aimed at providing convenient, private psychological support and emotional management tools.

  • Emotion Tracking: Tracking user emotional changes through voice and text analysis
  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy: AI-assisted cognitive behavioral therapy exercises
  • Meditation Guidance: Personalized meditation and relaxation training
  • Professional Connection: Recommending and connecting professional counselors when needed

Digisoul

Digisoul is an innovative Web3 Digital Nomad Mirror platform that transforms users' on-chain behaviors into personalized digital identities.

  • AI Avatar Generation: Automatic generation of personalized virtual avatars based on on-chain behavior
  • Virtual Group Photo System: Users can take group photos with other nomads' virtual avatars
  • AI Creation Studio: Generating derivative artworks based on natural language descriptions
